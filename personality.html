My Personality

My personality plays an important role in my academic life. The traits that best describe me are determination, curiosity, and responsibility. These traits help me stay focused, learn new skills, and manage my tasks such as my duties at work or my schoolwork.

My Top Three Personality Traits

1. Determination

Determination helps me continue working even when a task becomes difficult. In school, this trait helps me complete assignments, study for exams, and keep improving my technical skills. At work this helps me to strive to learn new concepts.

2. Curiosity

Curiosity makes me want to understand how technology works. This supports my academic life because I ask questions, research topics, and practise beyond what is taught in class.

3. Responsibility

Responsibility helps me manage deadlines, attend classes, and take my education seriously. It also helps me work well with others during group projects. It also helps me to manage my tasks at work.

AI Business Idea Review

Favourite Idea: AI for fraud detection and prevention in banking

My favourite AI business idea is AI for fraud detection and prevention in banking. This type of system can help banks monitor transactions, detect suspicious activity, identify unusual spending patterns, and alert security teams before customers lose money.

This idea can be applied in my world because I am interested in cybersecurity, technology, and protecting digital systems. AI fraud detection could help me better understand how banks protect customers from scams, identity theft, stolen card details, and unauthorized transactions. It would also be useful in Jamaica because more people are using online banking, mobile wallets, and digital payment services.

This AI business idea would be useful because fraud can happen very quickly, and human workers may not be able to manually review every transaction in real time. An AI system can analyze large amounts of transaction data, compare customer behaviour, and identify activities that do not look normal. This can help banks prevent financial losses, protect customers, and improve trust in digital banking services.

One ethical concern is privacy. An AI fraud detection system may need access to sensitive customer information, such as transaction history, location, account activity, and spending behaviour. If this data is not handled properly, it could expose customers to security and privacy risks.

Another ethical concern is unfair decision-making. If the AI system is trained on biased or incomplete data, it may wrongly flag innocent customers as suspicious. This could cause embarrassment, blocked transactions, or delays when customers are trying to access their money.

My solution to these concerns would be to use strong data protection practices such as encryption, access control, limited data collection, and clear customer consent. Banks should also regularly test the AI system for bias and make sure that human staff can review important decisions before action is taken against a customer.

An example of an actual application in this area is Feedzai .

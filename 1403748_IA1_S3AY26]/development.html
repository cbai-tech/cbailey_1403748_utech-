Personal Development

This section reflects on Chapters 4 to 7 of Twelve Pillars by Jim Rohn and Chris Widener. These chapters focus on personal growth, goal setting, time management, and relationships.

Chapter 4 Reflection

Achieve Your Goals

Chapter 4 teaches that goals are important because they give direction to a person’s life. Without goals, it is easy to become distracted or lose focus. Goals help people decide what they want and what actions they need to take.

I can apply this chapter to my life by setting clear academic and career goals. For example, I can set goals for improving my programming skills, completing assignments early, and gaining practical cybersecurity experience.

Chapter 5 Reflection

The Proper Use of Time

Chapter 5 focuses on the importance of time management. Time is valuable because once it is lost, it cannot be replaced. The chapter encourages readers to use time wisely and avoid wasting it on activities that do not support their goals.

I can apply this lesson by creating a study schedule, reducing distractions, and prioritising important tasks. Managing my time better will help me balance school, personal responsibilities, and skill development.

Chapter 6 Reflection

Surround Yourself with the Best People

Chapter 6 explains that the people around us can influence our thinking, habits, and success. Positive and focused people can encourage growth, while negative influences can slow progress.

I can apply this chapter by surrounding myself with people who are serious about education, personal growth, and career success. Working with motivated classmates can help me stay focused and improve academically.

Chapter 7 Reflection

Be a Life-Long Learner

Chapter 7 highlights the importance of continuous learning. Learning should not stop after school or university. To grow, a person must keep reading, practising, asking questions, and gaining new experiences.

I can apply this lesson by continuing to learn about cybersecurity, networking, programming, and professional development. Since technology changes quickly, lifelong learning is important for my future career.
